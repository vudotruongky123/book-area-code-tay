# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Sinh vien, nhan vien van phong, va nguoi doc thuong xuyen muon doc sach tren dien thoai hoac laptop ma khong bi ngat mach. Ho thuong tim mot noi de kham pha sach nhanh, doc tiep lien mach, luu danh sach ca nhan va cam thay viec doc de quay lai moi ngay.

## Product Purpose
Nen tang nay giup nguoi dung tim, doc, va duy tri thoi quen doc sach truc tuyen trong mot khong gian nhe, ro, va dang tin. Thanh cong cua trang chu la khien khach moi hieu ngay gia tri, muon thu doc ngay, va cam thay day la mot thu vien so co gu.

## Brand Personality
Tinh te, sang sua, binh than. Giong noi nen gan goi nhung co chon loc, uu tien cam giac yen tam, tri thuc, va de tiep can hon la khoa truong cong nghe.

## Anti-references
Khong giong landing page SaaS khuon mau voi nhieu the thong ke, gradient tim, va card lap lai. Khong theo huong hoc thuat kho gan, qua nhieu chu nho, hoac bo cuc nhu nha sach cu tren web 1.0. Khong dung visual on ao, qua nhieu hieu ung, hoac icon minh hoa chung chung.

## Design Principles
1. Doc la trung tam: moi section phai lam ro mot khoanh khac cua hanh vi doc, tu kham pha den tiep tuc doc.
2. Binh than nhung co sac thai: giao dien sang, sach, nhieu khoang tho, nhung van co mau xanh du de tao ban sac.
3. Goi mo tri thuc bang vat the that: uu tien hinh anh, copy, va texture lien quan den sach, tay, giay, anh sang ban ngay.
4. Dan duong ro rang: nguoi moi phai hieu ngay can bam dau, can kham pha gi, va loi ich khi dang ky.
5. Dang tin hon la khoa truong: thong diep, thanh phan, va chuyen dong phai gon, cham, va chac.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Huong toi WCAG AA cho do tuong phan, focus state ro rang, va text de doc tren mobile. Han che motion trang tri, ton trong `prefers-reduced-motion`, va khong dua thong tin quan trong chi bang mau sac.
